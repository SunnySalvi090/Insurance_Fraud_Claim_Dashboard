## üìù Project Overview
This project is a fully interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze insurance claims, detect potential fraud patterns, and present business-ready insights for finance and insurance companies such as **Marsh McLennan**, **Deutsche Bank**, and other risk-focused firms.  
The dashboard highlights trends in claims, fraud percentage, severity categories, policy types, and incident distributions using real-world synthetic insurance data.

---

## üéØ Purpose of the Project
Insurance companies face challenges in:
- Identifying **fraudulent claims**  
- Understanding **claim severity** and incident behavior  
- Monitoring **policy performance**  
- Improving **data-driven decision-making**

This dashboard solves these problems by using:
- Cleaned and modeled data  
- DAX-driven KPIs  
- Trend analyses  
- Interactive slicers  
- Fraud detection logic

## ‚Ä¢ Business Problem
- Insurance companies face difficulty tracking claim trends and identifying high-risk customer segments.
- Limited visibility into claim status, patterns, and processing efficiency affects decision-making.

## ‚Ä¢ Goal of Dashboard
- Deliver a unified view of claim performance and customer behavior.
- Identify trends in claim approval, rejection, and claim amount to support better risk assessment.

## ‚Ä¢ Key Visuals
- KPIs: Total Claims, Approved Claims, Rejected Claims, Average Claim Amount.
- Bar Chart: Claims by Age Group / Gender.
- Pie Chart: Claim Status Distribution.
- Line Chart: Monthly Claim Trends.
- Table: Customer-level and policy-level insights.

## ‚Ä¢ Business Impact & Insights
- Identified customer groups contributing to high claim losses.
- Improved understanding of approval/rejection patterns for better underwriting.
- Revealed seasonal or monthly claim spikes for resource planning.
- Enhanced transparency for stakeholders using clean and interactive visuals.
